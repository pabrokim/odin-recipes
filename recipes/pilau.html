Pilau

Description

Pilau rice is a simple dish of rice flavored with beef and spices . It is commonly served during special occasions such as Christmas and Eid. It normally taken as a standalone meal although it can be paired with tomato and cucumber salad.

Pilau dish is common in East Africa, India, and the Gulf countries. In East Africa, pilau is recognized as Swahili pilau. It has a distinct light brown color. The Indian version of pilau blends a lot of local influences which gives it a bright yellow appearance. The East African pilau is similar in appearance to the Gulf version of pilau

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg Basmati rice
  • 1/2kg beef
  • 4 cups hot water
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 spoons pilau spices
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 fresh ginger crushed
  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • Salt pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil beef with ginger for 10 minutes
  2. Heat oil, fry onions till light brown, add garlic and Pilau spices for 1 minute
  3. Add tomatoes, cook till tender
  4. Add rice, hot water then stir the mixture
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Cover the pot and cook on medium heat for 20 minutes
  7. Cover Pilau with aluminum foil and cook for 10 minutes
  8. Serve pilau while hot
